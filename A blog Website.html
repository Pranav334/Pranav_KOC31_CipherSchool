About

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃, Kimetsu no Yaiba, "Blade of Demon Destruction") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows teenage Tanjiro Kamado, who strives to become a demon slayer after his family was slaughtered and his younger sister, Nezuko, turned into a demon.

Season 1

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba’s first season aired from April 6, 2019, to September 28, 2019, in Japan. The English episodes were broadcast from October 13, 2019, to May 3, 2020.There were total 26 numbers of episode in first season.

Season 2

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba’s second season was split into two arcs. The Mugen Train Arc aired from October 10, 2021, to November 2021, while the Entertainment District Arc was broadcast from December 5 to February 13, 2022.
